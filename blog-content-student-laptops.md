## Why Students Need a Balanced Laptop (Not the Flashiest One)

Students don't usually need a high-end machine with crazy specs. What they do need is a laptop that won't act up during important moments — whether it's a Zoom class, a Canva assignment, a coding lab, or even basic research.

A good student laptop under 70k should meet these criteria:

- Fast enough for everyday academic tasks
- Stable and durable, so it lasts a few years
- Comfortable keyboard, especially for long typing sessions
- Decent battery backup, because students are always on the move
- Reliable thermals, so the laptop doesn't heat up like a stove
- Support for modern apps like MS Office, Zoom, Google Classroom, Canva, SPSS, basic coding tools, etc.

Surprisingly, many new laptops under this budget struggle with performance because brands compromise on build quality, RAM, or processor strength. That's why business-series used laptops have become a popular choice—they offer stronger performance at much lower prices.

## Used vs New Student Laptops (Under 70k)

Both used and new laptops have pros and cons, but when it comes to finding good student laptops under 70k in Pakistan, you actually get far better performance in the used business category.

### Used Business Laptops Include:

- **HP EliteBook 840 G6/G7**
- **Dell Latitude 5490 / 5500**
- **Lenovo ThinkPad T480 / T490**

These laptops often come with Core i5 or i7 processors, 8–16GB RAM, SSD storage, and strong durability. A new laptop in this same price range rarely offers similar power.

### On the other hand, brand-new laptops under 70k usually offer:

- Lower-end Celeron or Pentium processors
- Plastic bodies
- 4GB RAM (bare minimum, often limiting)
- Slower performance under multitasking

So if you want smoother performance and long-term usage, used business laptops give the best value.

But if you want the peace of mind that comes with a brand-new sealed device, and your tasks are lightweight, new budget laptops are still an option.
