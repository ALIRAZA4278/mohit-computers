What Actually Makes a Laptop “AI-Integrated”?
A lot of laptops claim to support AI workloads, but only a few genuinely do. A real AI-ready laptop must have:
•	A professional-grade GPU
•	A multi-core, high-speed processor
•	Fast SSD + enough RAM
•	A cooling system that prevents throttling
•	Hardware stability under load
Consumer laptops with basic GPUs can’t handle modern AI tools well — they slow down fast. That’s why workstation laptops are becoming the preferred choice in Pakistan.
